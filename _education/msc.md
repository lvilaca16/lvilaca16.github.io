## Curricular Projects
- 09/2020: Towards a Scalable Dataset Construction for Facial, A guided data selection approach for diversity stimulation:
    The work developed in this dissertation aims at investigating the impact of selecting a reduced amount of images in a Facial Recognition, when using Deep Learning. The solution proposed uses a feature engineering pipeline to discriminate the diversity of faces by increasing the amount of information. The goal is to maintain the algorithm's throughput while reducing the amount of images used.
