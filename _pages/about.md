👋 Hi, my name is Luís. I'm a research scholar at [INESC TEC](https://www.inesctec.pt/). I am working at INESC TEC's Centre for Telecommunications and Multimedia, more precisely in the Multimedia Communications Technologies research team.

📚 I completed my BSc and MSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the [School of Engineering, Polytechnic Institute of Porto (ISEP)](https://www.isep.ipp.pt). I am currently pursuing a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the [Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto](https://sigarra.up.pt/feup/en).

🖥️ During my time as a research assistant at INESC TEC, I was involved in three different Multimedia-related projects, exploring emergent concepts and tools of CV and ML for various applications. These contributions are documented in several technical reports and conference papers (core A*, B). I also organised workshops, co-supervised master theses, mentored summer internships, and presented ongoing work for dissemination purposes in international project meetings. In parallel, I was an assistant professor at ISEP, teaching computational systems, data communications and multimedia classes. I also did two internships at the [National Institute of Informatics (NII)](https://www.nii.ac.jp/en/) (one of them on-site), where I worked on multimodal machine learning, representation learning for audio-visual data, and cross-modal retrieval. I am currently in the middle of a one-year abroad period, working at NII as a visiting researcher, which is part of my PhD Program. Some details are available in the [Experience section](/experience/).

🔬 My research is focused on deep learning applied to multimodal learning, specifically audio-visual data. My goal is to find a bridge between structured knowledge and deep networks. Deep learning still ignores explicit knowledge about the research problem at hand. In particular, I am interested in applying self-supervised learning to learn from unlabeled audio-visual data. Additionally, I am also interested in multimedia recommendation. Some details are available in the [Publications section](/publications/).

🎮🎾 During my free time, I enjoy doing sports, photography, and gaming. I also play Padel with my colleagues from INESC TEC occasionally.
