**Description** Developing and maintaining the computational structure of CTM for its researchers (DevOps/MLOps)


## Description

During this time I developed from root the computational infrastructure for ML-based experiments within CTM. The infrastructure was based on [Slurm](https://github.com/SchedMD/slurm). I was in contact with HPC systems, virtualization technologies, e.g. Docker, VirtualBox, Hyper-V, VMWare, and IaC/S software, e.g. Ansible and OpenStack. The project was entirely automated using Gitlab CI/CD mechanisms for generating new Docker images and pushing changes into production automatically.
